About

We are a nonprofit student-led organization with the goal of providing free hackathons for youth looking to expand their experience and knowledge of coding. NextUp holds hackathons in the programming language, python, for students from 1st to 10th grade. We also hold a hackathon marathon for more advanced coders. Click the Hackathon Registration tab above to learn about out hackathons and hackathon marathons!

Students can register for 3 different hackathon difficulty levels: beginner, intermediate, and challenging. Register yourself or your child for a hackathon today by clicking registration at the top right of you screen, free of charge! Hackathon marathons have a registration fee of $5
