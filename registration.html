How do our hackathons work?

Each competition lasts one day. Hackers will write a Python program in response to the prompt given to them. Each competition level receives a different prompt. After the day ends, submissions are judged anonymously and fairly. Winners are announced after one week.

Prizes

  • Beginner Level: Official NextUp Certificate
  • Intermediate Level: Official NextUp Certificate
  • Challenging Level: Official NextUp Certificate

100% Free!
